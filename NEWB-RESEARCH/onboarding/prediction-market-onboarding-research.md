# Prediction Market Onboarding Research: Beginner Entry & Experience

Research Date: December 25, 2025
Focus: How complete beginners enter and get started with prediction markets

---

## Executive Summary

This research identifies the major pathways for beginners to enter prediction markets, analyzing current platforms, onboarding flows, barriers to entry, and best practices from adjacent industries (trading/betting apps).

**Key Finding**: There is a stark divide between two types of prediction market platforms:
1. **Fiat-based, regulated platforms** (Kalshi) - Low barrier to entry, traditional signup
2. **Crypto-based platforms** (Polymarket) - High technical barrier, requires wallet/connection

---

## 1. Entry Methods & Platforms

### 1.1 Platforms That Don't Require Crypto Wallets

#### **Kalshi** (Top Recommendation for Beginners)
- **Type**: CFTC-regulated prediction market
- **Entry Method**: Traditional signup with email/SSN
- **Funding**: Fiat currency only
- **Geographic**: Originally US-only, expanding to 140+ countries (Oct 2025)
- **Minimum Deposit**: $1 (ACH)
- **No Crypto Wallet Required**: ✓ Yes

**Why Best for Beginners:**
- Most frequently mentioned across 2025 guides
- Regulated by CFTC (trust factor)
- Accepts traditional payment methods
- Low minimum deposit ($1)
- No technical blockchain knowledge needed

**URL**: https://kalshi.com

---

#### **Robinhood**
- **Type**: Traditional brokerage with prediction markets
- **Entry Method**: Standard brokerage account signup
- **Funding**: Bank account link
- **No Crypto Wallet Required**: ✓ Yes

**Beginner Advantages:**
- Familiar interface to many users
- Zero-commission structure
- Progressive complexity disclosure

---

#### **Fanatics Markets & Underdog**
- **Type**: Newer prediction market entrants
- **Entry Method**: Traditional app signup
- **Funding**: Traditional payment methods

### 1.2 Platforms Requiring Crypto Wallets

#### **Polymarket** (Most Popular Crypto Platform)
- **Type**: Decentralized prediction market
- **Entry Method**: Wallet connection (MetaMask, Rabby, Phantom)

- **Funding**: Virtual currency only

## 2. First-Time User Experiences

### 2.1 Kalshi Onboarding Flow

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Account Creation**
   - Email signup or Google sign-in
   - KYC verification (SSN, identity verification)
   - Similar to opening any brokerage account

2. **Funding Account**
   - Link bank account (ACH transfer)
   - Minimum deposit: $1
   - Processing time: 2-4 business days
   - Alternative: Wire transfer ($1,000 minimum)

3. **First Trade Tutorial**
   - Official guide: "Picking Your First Market"
   - Explains event contracts concept
   - Shows order book and pricing
   - Walks through placing first trade

**Educational Resources:**
- Official blog: Tips and tricks for understanding contracts
- YouTube tutorials: Live trading demonstrations
- Beginner guides: Strategy frameworks
- Community: Reddit guides and strategies

**URLs:**
- Onboarding: https://help.kalshi.com
- First Market Guide: https://news.kalshi.com/p/picking-your-first-market
- Tutorial: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lmmgVZscZso

---

### 2.2 Polymarket Onboarding Flow

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Wallet Setup** (High Friction)
   - Install MetaMask browser extension
   - Create or import wallet
   - Secure seed phrase (critical step)
   - Switch to Polygon network

2. **Acquire USDC** (Additional Step)
   - Buy USDC from exchange (Coinbase, etc.)
   - Transfer to MetaMask wallet
   - Understanding gas fees

3. **Connect & Sign**
   - Connect wallet to Polymarket
   - Sign message to authenticate
   - No email/password required

4. **First Prediction**
   - Browse markets by category
   - Understand odds/probabilities
   - Place first bet using USDC

**Educational Resources:**
- Official docs: How to sign up
- Video tutorials: Step-by-step wallet setup
- Community guides: Reddit strategies

**URLs:**
- Official Docs: https://docs.polymarket.com/polymarket-learn/get-started/how-to-signup
- Wallet Guide: https://polymarketguide.gitbook.io/polymarketguide/basics/signing-up/sign-up-methods
- Tutorial: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TujPaMc0yv0

---

### 2.3 Manifold Markets Onboarding Flow

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Account Creation**
   - Simple email signup
   - No KYC required (play money)

2. **Receive Mana**
   - Initial allocation of virtual currency

3. **Start Trading**

## 3. Barriers to Entry (Non-Tech Users)

### 3.1 Crypto Wallet Requirements

**High Barrier Platforms:**
- **Polymarket**: Requires MetaMask installation and setup
- **Augur**: Full crypto wallet management
- **Gnosis**: Wallet connection required

**Challenges:**
- Understanding seed phrases and security
- Network switching (mainnet vs. Polygon)
- Gas fees and transaction costs
- USDC acquisition before trading

**Low Barrier Platforms:**
- **Kalshi**: No wallet required
- **Robinhood**: Traditional account setup
- **Manifold**: Email signup only

---

### 3.2 Understanding Odds & Probabilities

**Educational Resources Found:**
- MetaMask prediction market concepts guide
- Beginner's guides to prediction market terminology
- Strategy frameworks for understanding pricing

**Key Concepts to Explain:**
- Binary options (Yes/No outcomes)
- Probability pricing (e.g., 65¢ = 65% probability)
- Order books and liquidity
- Selling positions early

**URLs:**
- Concepts Guide: https://metamask.io/news/prediction-markets-concepts-terminology
- Beginner Guide: https://changehero.io/blog/crypto-prediction-markets-guide/

---

### 3.3 Funding Accounts

**Fiat On-Ramps (Kalshi):**
- **ACH Transfer**: $1 minimum, free, 2-4 days
- **Wire Transfer**: $1,000 minimum, bank fees apply
- **Debit Cards**: Available (limits vary)

**Crypto On-Ramps (Polymarket):**
- **USDC Required**: Must acquire first
- **Exchanges**: Coinbase, Kraken, etc.
- **Additional Steps**: Buy crypto → transfer to wallet → trade

**Major Difference:**
- Kalshi: 1 step (link bank)
- Polymarket: 3+ steps (exchange → wallet → platform)

---

### **Kalshi Geographic Availability** (2025):
- **Original**: US-only (CFTC regulated)
- **Expansion**: 140+ countries (October 2025)
- **Restricted**: Various African nations, sanctioned jurisdictions

**Key Insight**: Geographic fragmentation is a major barrier - many users cannot access popular platforms due to local regulations.

## 5. Progressive Disclosure & Complexity Management

### 5.1 Core Principles

**From Fintech Best Practices:**
1. **Show value immediately** before asking for commitment
2. **Establish trust** from the first tap
3. **Progressive disclosure** - don't overwhelm with features
4. **Behavioral triggers** for personalized flows
5. **Context-aware** interfaces

### 5.2 First Trade Experience Design

**What Makes Good First Trade Experience:**
- Clear call-to-action
- Low stakes (minimum deposit or demo mode)
- Instant confirmation/feedback
- Celebration of completion
- Clear next steps
- Undo option (within reason)

**Examples:**
- Robinhood: Confetti animation on first trade
- Coinbase: Step-by-step buy tutorial
- Cash App: Simplified stock purchase flow

## 7. Key Insights for Dashboard Design

### 7.1 Beginner Pathways

**Recommended Entry Path:**

1. **Start with Play Money** (Manifold)
   - Learn mechanics risk-free
   - Understand probability pricing
   - Build confidence

2. **Progress to Fiat Platform** (Kalshi)
   - Low minimum ($1)
   - Regulated and trustworthy
   - Traditional signup flow

3. **Optional: Advanced Crypto** (Polymarket)
   - Only if interested in crypto
   - Higher technical barrier
   - More market variety

---

### 7.2 Onboarding Checklist

**Essential Elements:**
- ✓ Simple signup (email/social)
- ✓ Clear value proposition (what is this?)
- ✓ Low first-trade barrier ($1-10)
- ✓ Interactive tutorial/walkthrough
- ✓ Practice mode or demo
- ✓ Progress indicators
- ✓ Instant feedback on actions
- ✓ Clear explanations of odds/pricing
- ✓ Undo/cancel options
- ✓ Help resources accessible

---

### 7.3 Common Barriers to Address

1. **"What is a prediction market?"**
   - Clear explanation upfront
   - Simple examples
   - Comparison to familiar concepts (betting, stock trading)

2. **"How do I read the odds?"**
   - Visual probability displays
   - Hover tooltips
   - Example calculations

3. **"Is this safe?"**
   - Regulation information
   - Security explanations
   - Risk disclaimers

4. **"How do I deposit money?"**
   - Step-by-step funding guide
   - Multiple payment options
   - Clear fee disclosure

5. **"Can I practice first?"**
   - Demo mode
   - Low minimum trades
   - Educational content

## 9. Conclusion

**Major Findings:**

1. **Fragmented Landscape**: Prediction markets are split between fiat-based (low barrier) and crypto-based (high barrier) platforms

2. **Geographic Restriction**: Significant barrier - many users blocked from major platforms

3. **Limited Practice Options**: Most platforms lack demo/practice modes

4. **Onboarding Variation**: Huge difference in onboarding complexity (Kalshi: traditional vs. Polymarket: technical)

5. **Best Practices Available**: Adjacent industries (trading, betting) have excellent onboarding patterns to emulate

**Recommendation for Dashboard:**
- Support multiple platform types
- Provide clear pathway recommendations
- Include practice/simulation mode
- Explain concepts progressively
- Address geographic limitations clearly
